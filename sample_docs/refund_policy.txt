=========================================
REFUND & RETURN POLICY
=========================================
Last Updated: July 2026

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not satisfied, we offer refunds and exchanges under the following conditions:

1. RETURN WINDOW:
Customers can request a refund or return within 30 days of the delivery date. Items returned after 30 days are not eligible for a refund.

2. ELIGIBILITY:
To be eligible for a return, the item must be unused, in the same condition that you received it, and in its original packaging. Gift cards and downloadable software products are non-returnable.

3. RESTOCKING FEES:
All electronic products are subject to a 10% restocking fee. Other goods (apparel, books) do not have restocking fees.

4. REFUND TIMELINE:
Once we receive and inspect your returned item, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. Approved refunds are processed back to the original payment method within 5 to 7 business days.

5. RETURN SHIPPING COSTS:
Customers are responsible for paying their own shipping costs for returning items unless the item arrived damaged or defective, in which case SupportAgent.ai will cover the return shipping label cost.
