=========================================
SHIPPING & DELIVERY POLICY
=========================================
Last Updated: July 2026

1. SHIPPING RATES:
- Standard Shipping: Free for orders over $50. Otherwise, a flat rate of $4.99 applies.
- Express Shipping: Flat rate of $14.99 for all orders.

2. DELIVERY ESTIMATES:
- Standard domestic shipping takes 3 to 5 business days.
- Express shipping takes 1 to 2 business days.
- We do not ship on Sundays or major public holidays.

3. INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING:
We currently ship to Canada, the United Kingdom, Germany, Australia, and Japan. International shipping rates are calculated at checkout and standard delivery takes 7 to 14 business days. Custom duties are the responsibility of the customer.

4. ORDER TRACKING:
You will receive a tracking link via email as soon as your order ships. Tracking numbers can take up to 24 hours to become active on the carrier's website.

5. LOST OR DAMAGED SHIPMENTS:
If your order is lost in transit or arrives damaged, please notify us within 48 hours of the scheduled delivery date. We will initiate a replacement order or credit your account.
